Passion Island catamaran excursion

Treat yourself to a fabulous experience in Cozumel's water. This catamaran tour offers the chance to explore and admire the wildlife of Ilha da Paixão, enjoy a service on board that includes a full lunch, with fresh fruit, rum punch and margaritas (you can also bring your own alcohol), and soft drinks. You can snorkel or paddle board, or just relax on the beach. You will sail for about 2 hours (1 hour to return / 1 hour to return), stay for up to 2.5 hours on the beach at Passion Island and, at the end, you can snorkel on the way back for about 30 minutes.

Water Park in Mr Sancho's Beach Club

For a fun day in Cozumel for all ages, head to the water park at Mr. Sancho's Beach Club to play with floating toys of all sizes. Dive into the water of floating towers, size inflatable icebergs, jump on trampolines, and close water slides. This enclosed water park in the shallow waters of the Caribbean Sea creates a safe and fun destination for children and adults.

Cozumel Beach with horseback riding

Get ready for a 30-minute adventure ride on Mr Sancho’s beach. Prepare yourself with a horse that matches your skill and follow the guide into the jungle, passing through mangrove forests and farms, before reaching the stunning coastline of white sand beaches and the sparkling blue waters of Cozumel. This tour for the whole family is suitable for riders of all levels.
