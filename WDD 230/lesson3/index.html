Site Name and Domain Name

The site name will be Don't Rain ON My Parade and the domain name will be dontrain.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site will be to assist people who work in professions that require them to be on the street constantly. These types of jobs have the need for the person to know the weather forecast before planning their work day. If it's a rainy day they need to have an umbrella, if it's a sunny one they need a sun glasses and sunscreen and etc ...
