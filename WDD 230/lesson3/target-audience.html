Target Audience

The target audience are self-employed professionals in the door-to-door sales area, telephone maintenance technicians who need to work with switching wires on street poles, and professionals in the area of census and population research.

The age range of the public target is between 28 and 40 years

The motivation behind this target audience is to be a reference of reliable climate information for people who suffer the most from climate insiders.

Personas

Name: Jonas Franco

Profession: Perfume seller

Age: 35

Motivation: Jonas wants to make a good money with door-to-door sales to have money to pay for your law school and become a lawyer.

Technology: In his sales he uses a smartphone to access his list of customers and his sales route. He also uses his smartphone to show products to potential customers.

Name: Anna Lee

Profession: Electrical Technician

Age: 25

Motivation: Anna Lee wants to get a good experience in the field of electrical engineering to be able to use your electrical engineering degree to work in a large power generation company.

Technology: In addition to her electrical installation and maintenance equipment, Anna Lee uses a tablet granted by the company to know her maintenance route and to respond to emergency maintenance calls.
